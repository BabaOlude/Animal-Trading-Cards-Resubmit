Elephant

Elephants have the ability to identify themselves in a mirror.

  • Mass: Typical mass of Elephants - African bush elephant: 13,000 lbs, Asian elephant: 12,000 lbs, African forest elephant: 6,000 lbs
  • Gestation>: Typical gestation period of an Elephant - African bush elephant: 22 months, Asian elephant: 18 – 22 months
  • Lifespan: Typical Lifespan of an Elephant - African bush elephant: 60 – 70 years, Asian elephant: 48 years, African forest elephant: 60 – 70 years
  • Height: Typical Height of an Elephant - African bush elephant: 11 ft., Asian elephant: 8.9 ft.

Elephants are extremely intelligent animals and have memories that span many years. It is this memory that serves matriarchs well during dry seasons when they need to guide their herds, sometimes for tens of miles, to watering holes that they remember from the past. They also display signs of grief, joy, anger and play.
